ICO Marketing

Initial Coin Offering (ICO) is a crowdfunding method startups use to raise capital for their new cryptocurrency-based projects. In simple terms, it is a form of fundraising where investors can purchase digital tokens in exchange for traditional cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum. At Zemax, we help ICOs reach their fundraising goals by delivering creative and effective crypto ICO marketing solutions.

IDO Marketing Services

Initial DEX Offering (IDO) marketing involves tailored promotional strategies to launch and promote a token on a decentralized exchange. It includes a number of key strategies to attract potential investors, increase token visibility, and generate buzz around the token offering.

IEO Marketing Services

The way you market your IEO (Initial Exchange Offering) can make or break a project's success. Our IEO marketing services provide you with the expert strategies, guidance, and support you need to reach your fundraising goals and get your project off the ground.

DeFi Marketing Solutions

The rise of decentralized finance (DeFi) has been swift and unprecedented. DeFi projects have captured the attention of investors and traders alike, with billions of dollars being locked into various protocols. DeFi marketing is the process of promoting and creating awareness of these projects and what they offer.

NFT Marketing Solutions

NFT marketing and promotion are essential for promoting and selling your NFTs in a highly competitive marketplace. At Coinband, we offer a range of NFT marketing solutions to help you bring your NFT project to the forefront of the market.

GameFi Marketing

Your GameFi project deserves a marketing approach that is tailored specifically for the gaming community. Our solutions will help you build your brand, create buzz and attract the right audience for your GameFi project.
